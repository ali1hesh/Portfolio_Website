    Project Trainee

    National Thermal Power Corporation Ltd.

    During my tenure as a project trainee at NTPC Ltd., I had the opportunity to delve into the operations of a 660MW supercritical unit, a sophisticated component in contemporary power generation. My training provided a comprehensive examination of the entire power generation process, from coal handling to the delivery of electricity over long transmission lines.

    I gained valuable insights into the operation and maintenance of high-pressure boilers, which are pivotal in generating the steam required for power generation. I was involved in monitoring boiler performance, ensuring optimal combustion processes, and addressing challenges related to thermal efficiency and emissions control.

    Additionally, my experience included understanding the integration of advanced technologies designed to manage and distribute the generated power efficiently over long transmission lines. This aspect of the training highlighted the importance of minimizing energy losses and maintaining stability in the power grid.

    Overall, my time at NTPC Ltd. offered a thorough understanding of modern power generation technologies and underscored the significance of efficiency, sustainability, and innovation in meeting the growing global demand for electricity.
